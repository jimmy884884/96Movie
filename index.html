96 - Tamil Movie

A romantic drama featuring Vijay Sethupathi and Trisha Krishnan

About the Movie

Director: C. Prem Kumar

Release Date: October 3, 2018

Runtime: 158 minutes

Genres: Romance, Drama

Plot Summary: The story follows Ram and Jaanu, who were deeply in love during their school days. After years apart, they meet again at a reunion, leading to a journey filled with memories, emotions, and reflections on their past relationship.

Cast

  • Vijay Sethupathi as Ram
  • Trisha Krishnan as Jaanu
  • Supporting Cast: Devadarshini, Aadukalam Naren, and others.

Important Movie Trivia

  • The film was a major box office success and received critical acclaim.
  • Vijay Sethupathi and Trisha Krishnan's performances were highly praised.
  • The film's music was composed by Govind Vasantha, which became very popular.
  • It was remade in Telugu as "Jaanu" in 2020.
  • The film explores themes of nostalgia and the impact of first love.
